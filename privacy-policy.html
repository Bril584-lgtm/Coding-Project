Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTok Auto Poster application ("the App") handles information. The App is a personal automation tool used solely by its developer to post AI-generated motivational quote videos to a single TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from third parties. The only data handled by the App is:

  • TikTok OAuth access tokens for the app owner's account, stored locally on the developer's machine.
  • Video files generated locally before upload.

2. How We Use Information

Access tokens are used exclusively to authenticate API calls to TikTok's Content Posting API on behalf of the app owner. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API — used to upload videos to the app owner's account.
  • Google Text-to-Speech (gTTS) — used to generate voiceover audio.
  • ElevenLabs (optional) — used for premium AI voiceover generation.

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

No user data is retained beyond what is necessary to operate the App. Access tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the .env file.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
